Individual Assignments



This section documents all individual assignments from the HCI4Business course. Each assignment follows the human-centered design methodology: Understand, Observe, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. Key deliverables include user research with 25 Italian homeowners, detailed personas (Claudio the Record-Keeper, Marco), Lean Canvas models, competitive analysis, and cost breakdowns. The individual work demonstrates research rigor within academic constraints. Each deliverable connects to the next, building a complete picture of human-centered innovation in the smart security domain.

Tools used: Google Forms for surveys, Gamma, Beautiful.ai, Tome, and Canva for presentations. Market research drew from NeighborhoodScout, Amazon reviews, Italian security forums, and EU regulatory documents. These assignments form the foundation for the Volturio group project.
